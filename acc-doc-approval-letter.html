Dear Mr. Bravo,

The Willow Creek HOA Architectural Review Committee has completed its review of your request, received on April 7, 2025, for a mailbox replacement at the above-referenced property. After careful evaluation against the Association's CC&Rs and Architectural Guidelines, the Committee has reached the following decision:

APPROVED WITH STIPULATIONS

Your request is conditionally approved subject to strict compliance with all of the following stipulations:

  1. The replacement mailbox must be installed in a matte black powder-coated finish only. No bronze, brushed nickel, or decorative finishes are permitted on Hurricane Shoals Road.
  2. Final unit dimensions must not exceed 14" in width or 10" in height, as established in Section 4.7 of the CC&Rs.
  3. The mailbox post must be set at exactly 42 inches from finished grade to the bottom of the mailbox door opening.
  4. House numbers must be placed on the left side of the mailbox door using black vinyl numerals, consistent with adjacent properties.
  5. Installation must be completed by a licensed contractor. License number must be on file with property management prior to work commencing.
  6. All work must be completed within 60 calendar days of this letter's date. Failure to complete within this window will require resubmission.

Please retain a copy of this letter and present it to your contractor prior to installation. The Committee reserves the right to request a post-installation inspection to verify compliance with the above conditions.

Should you have any questions regarding these stipulations or the installation requirements, please contact your community manager Carol Green at management@willowcreekhoa.org or call (770) 555-0182.

On behalf of the Willow Creek HOA Architectural Review Committee,

Dalton Thomson Committee Chair · Willow Creek HOA Board of Directors
Important: This approval is for the scope of work described in Request #ACC-2025-0412 only. Any modifications beyond the described scope require a new ACC submission. This document does not constitute approval for any work other than the mailbox replacement as specified above.
