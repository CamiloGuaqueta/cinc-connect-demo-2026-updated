Violation Items

Fencing

Unauthorized Fencing

A wooden privacy fence was erected along the rear and right side property lines without prior ACC approval. Installed in March 2025 with no submitted request form or board consent. The structure is visible from the community walking path and has generated complaints from adjacent residents.

Fence Height Violation

The installed fence measures 6 feet 2 inches at its tallest point, exceeding the community maximum of 4 feet per Section 3.4 of the CC&Rs. Non-compliant height is concentrated near the alley gate and the northeast corner of the lot, creating a visible obstruction from the common area walking path.

Fence Color Non-Compliance

The fence is painted in a dark burgundy tone not included in the Association's approved exterior color palette. Section 5.1 of the Architectural Guidelines requires natural wood stain or an approved earth tone. No color change approval was submitted or granted prior to painting.
